About Us

At Dr. Borah Health Clinic, we specialize in advanced skin, hair, and aesthetic treatments tailored to individual needs. With a commitment to clinical excellence and compassionate care, we bring the latest dermatological innovations to every patient we serve.

  • Clinical Dermatology

    Diagnosis and treatment of skin diseases with medical precision and expertise.

  • Hair Transplantation & PRP Therapy

    Restoring natural hair growth through modern transplant techniques and platelet-rich plasma therapy.

  • Aesthetic Dermatology

    Enhancing skin health and appearance with treatments for glow, texture, and anti-aging.

  • Dermatosurgery

    Minor surgical procedures for skin tags, moles, cysts, and other skin growths.

  • Laser Hair Removal

    Safe and effective permanent hair reduction using advanced laser technology.
